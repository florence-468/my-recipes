Porridge Recipes

Description

Ingredients

  • 2Kg/ 2000g peeled white yam
  • Half cup of ground crayfish
  • 1 seasoning cubes.
  • 150 ml of palm oil.
  • Chilli pepper (to taste)
  • Small ground fresh Peppers
  • 1 cup of Sliced Onions

Steps

  1. Peel off the bark of the yam, slice to serve-able sizes, wash and set aside. Also slice or grind the tomatoes, fresh pepper and a cup of crayfish.
  2. Pour 150ml of palm oil into a cooking pot, allow to heat and bleach a little (about two minutes). Add half of the sliced onions. Pour in the ground tomatoes/peppers.
  3. Fry the ground tomatoes/pepper and onions for five to ten minutes, add water and transfer the sliced yams. Add water so that the yam pieces are almost submerged in water (but not completely submerged). Allow boiling then add crayfish, a seasoning cube, salt and allow to cook for the next ten minutes.
  4. Add the remaining sliced onions. Cover and allow to cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. You can pierce with a cooking fork to know if the yam is soft enough for consumption. You can add vegetable, it is very nice for porridge yam even though I omitted it while making the delicious recipe above.
  6. Cook for another three to five minutes and you just made my favorite yam porridge. Stir all together, keep stirring till it thickens.
